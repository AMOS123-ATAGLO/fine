Problem Identified

University students struggle to afford textbooks, lab materials, and supplies. These are expensive, hard to resell, and often go to waste.

Our Solution

EduConnect is a student-friendly app and website where students can buy, sell, or rent academic resources such as textbooks, calculators, lab coats, and more — directly from each other.

Why EduKipsa Is Unique

  • Campus-specific marketplace
  • Affordable rentals for a semester
  • QR codes for easy listings
  • Condition rating system
  • Delivery or campus pickup
  • Wallet for secure payments
  • Ratings for both buyers and sellers
  • Option to donate books to students in need
